FAQs

How does Ecospark calculate my carbon footprint?
Ecospark uses standard emission factors for flights, electricity, and transport to estimate your footprint.
Can I edit my previous entries?
Yes, once logged in, you can view your history and update or delete old data entries.
How can I reduce my emissions effectively?
We provide personalized tips based on your activity, like switching to renewables and reducing flights.
